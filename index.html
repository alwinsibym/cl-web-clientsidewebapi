About Elon Musk & SpaceX

Elon Musk, a visionary entrepreneur, founded SpaceX in 2002 with the goal of reducing space transportation costs and enabling the colonization of Mars. Under his leadership, SpaceX has successfully developed the Falcon and Starship rockets, as well as the Dragon spacecraft. SpaceX became the first private company to send astronauts to the International Space Station.
